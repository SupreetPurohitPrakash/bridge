Our Story

Employment services needed an analytics tool and JN Solutions was founded with a product called ‘Starcast’

2008 Start Up

Our first product was a forecasting tool for ‘Star Ratings’.

2009 JSAdvantage

JSAdvantage launched combining analytics, Starcast and the first iteration of Bridge to support the Job Services Australia contract.

2012 JSAdvantage for DES.
The early years

Bridge is launched formally as a front- end UI to capture additional data and improve forecasting and a Disability Employment Services product is introduced to the market.

2015 CHIMPS is developed
Demand leads to development

As the system becomes more popular our provider partners need support to build efficiency into the challenging jobactive contract. Where no API is available our CHIMPS are able to integrate with ESSWeb and save valuable time by completing tasks automatically.

2016
IRAP

Bridge successfully completes the initial IRAP accreditation process including as a SaaS solution hosted in a secure, sovereign cloud.

2017
CARE DEVELOPMENT

Specific care features are added to the system to build on the employment services functionality.

2020 Participant landmark achieved.

Bridge proudly and securely supports over 1,000,000 participant records.

Today BRIDGE IS READY!

Bridge is ready for the future of employment services with game changing digital solutions.

Bridge is ready to synchronise with the NDIS myplace portal.

Bridge is ready to help your business succeed in a challenging environment.
